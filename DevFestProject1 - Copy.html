Homeless Care Package

If you are asking yourself: what can I do to help? Here's a few suggestions on helping your neighbors:

Creating and distributing Care Packages can foster a sense of community. Some necessities include: clean socks, toiletries, and nutritious food. Often, large amounts of food does not keep well, especially during the hot summer months. Consider donating giftcards of restaurants instead. That way, the person knows they have their next meal planned out.
